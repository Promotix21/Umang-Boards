Manufacturing Excellence

Where Precision
Meets Scale

Two state-of-the-art facilities with NABL accredited laboratories, operating under Triple ISO certification to deliver insulation products that meet the world's most demanding specifications.

Vertically Integrated Operations
From raw cellulose pulp to finished machined components — complete control over quality at every stage of production. Our in-house capabilities eliminate supply chain dependencies and ensure consistency across million-unit batches.
100%
NABL Accredited Laboratory
In-house testing to IEC/IES 17025 standards. Every batch undergoes dielectric strength, moisture content, density, and tensile testing before dispatch. Full traceability from raw material to delivery.
Large Format Pressboards
Capability to produce laminated pressboards up to 3200 × 2100mm for medium and power class transformers — one of very few manufacturers globally with this capacity.
3200mm
Custom Engineering
CNC precision machining for spacers, rings, caps, channels, and angles. Full CAD-to-production service with tolerances to ±0.1mm for critical insulation components.
±0.1mm
Non-Metallic Particle
India's sole manufacturer using non-metallic particle technology — eliminating conductive contamination risk, a critical requirement for high-voltage transformer insulation.
